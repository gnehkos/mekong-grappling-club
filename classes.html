No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu

Regular Class

Our regular No-Gi class is open to everyone, from complete beginners to experienced grapplers. No uniform needed — just a rash guard and shorts. We focus on learning through exploration, not memorisation. Each class includes positional sparring and cooperative drilling so you develop real understanding, not just moves.

Kids Class

Kids Class

A fun, safe, and structured class designed for children to learn the basics of grappling. We build confidence, discipline, and coordination through games and guided movement. Kids learn how to move their body, stay calm under pressure, and work respectfully with others — skills that go well beyond the mat.

Women's Only Class

A dedicated space for women to train in a comfortable and supportive environment. Whether you're brand new to grappling or looking to sharpen your skills, this class is built around your pace.
